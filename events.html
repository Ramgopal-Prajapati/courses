Introduction to Cloud computing

Date: Coming soon
Time:coming soon
Venue: Online ( zoom Meeting)
Hosted by: Ramgopal Prajapati


Step into the future of technology with our comprehensive Cloud Computing Workshop. This workshop is designed to provide you with a solid understanding of cloud computing concepts, tools, and applications.

What You Will Learn:


Introduction to Cloud Computing:
Understanding the basics and benefits of cloud computing.

Cloud Service Models:
Explore different service models including IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS.

Popular Cloud Platforms:
An overview of leading cloud platforms like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud.

Cloud Deployment Models:
Public, private, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments.

Cloud Security:
Best practices for securing data and applications in the cloud.

Hands-on Labs:
Practical exercises on deploying and managing cloud resources.

Who Should Attend:


IT professionals looking to expand their skillset. Students and enthusiasts eager to learn about cloud technologies. Businesses and startups interested in leveraging cloud solutions. What to Bring: A laptop with internet access. Basic understanding of IT and networking concepts (recommended but not mandatory).

Additional Information:


Refreshments will be provided. Participants will receive a certificate of completion. Networking opportunities with fellow attendees and industry professionals.

Introduction to Web Development

Date: Coming soon
Time:coming soon
Venue: Online ( zoom Meeting)
Hosted by: Ramgopal Prajapati


Are you eager to dive into the world of web development? Join us for an exciting and interactive workshop where you will learn the fundamentals of creating stunning and functional websites from scratch.

What You Will Learn:


Introduction to Web Development:
Understanding the basics of web technologies and how the internet works.

HTML & CSS:
Learn to structure your web pages with HTML and style them with CSS to create visually appealing layouts.

JavaScript Basics:
Add interactivity to your websites with JavaScript, making your pages dynamic and user-friendly.

Responsive Design:
Techniques to ensure your website looks great on all devices, from desktops to mobile phones.

Web Development Tools:
Overview of essential tools and resources for efficient web development.

Hands-on Projects:
Build your own web page from scratch and apply what you've learned in real-time.

Who Should Attend:


Beginners with no prior experience in web development. Aspiring web developers looking to strengthen their foundational skills. Anyone interested in understanding how websites are built and maintained. What to Bring: A laptop with internet access. A willingness to learn and explore new concepts.

Additional Information:


Refreshments will be provided. Participants will receive a certificate of completion. Networking opportunities with fellow attendees and industry professionals.
